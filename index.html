Our Heritage

Generations of Saffron Mastery

For over three generations, the Mitra family has cultivated the world's most precious spice in the pristine valleys of Kashmir. Our saffron is grown using traditional methods passed down through our ancestors, ensuring the highest quality and purity.

Each crimson stigma is hand-picked at dawn when the flowers are at their peak, preserving the delicate aroma and potent properties that make saffron the "Red Gold" of spices.

We are committed to sustainable farming practices that protect our environment while producing saffron with unparalleled flavor, color, and aroma.
